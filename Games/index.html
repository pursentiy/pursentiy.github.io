Some words about me and not just that

Hi! This page is all about gaming projects. Until now I have passed three courses already. While taking them the main purpose for me was not just to follow the teacher's instruction but learn from him and use that knowladge in multiple challenges either made by them or by my own. That's the only opportunity to learn I suppose. Besides that I was studying a lot how to write nice, clean code, trying to make it readable, simple and efficient at the same time. Thouht I have a lot ahead but i've already covered quite a lot and im trully happy about that.
I am taking 1st semester of masters degree at the Moscow Technical University (MIREA) after completing my bachelor in cybernetics.
Games have always been my passion. The older I get, the more I was involved in both playing and learning about how those intricate worlds were made. I recall my first experience with such discoveries, back in 2015 when I was learning C# at university and I found Unity engine. Although it was mostly for pleasure, I was eager to learn more. At the same time, my friend showed me another field: Web. For the next few years, I was creating sites and scripting. After some time, I moved on to and started my journey with Node.js, which was in fact the topic of my bachelor thesis. Looking further to develop my skills, I took an internship in Slovakia at Ness (Digital Engineering). This was not only a major boost for my code writing experience, team work and problem-solving skills, but I also gained an international perspective. During this time, I was writing an app, learning the basics of React and working on C# server, and further deepening my understanding of the field of games in my free time.
This experience was crucial for my career, as from that moment I knew that I want to become a game developer. Looking back, I see that I have done a lot, and sill have much to do, such as launching my first online game and other projects. Though it's a tricky route but it worths that. Thats my dream! I aim at my goal like i've never been before!
